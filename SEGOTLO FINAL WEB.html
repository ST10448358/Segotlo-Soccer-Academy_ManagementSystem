What We Do

Our Programmes

A holistic approach to youth development — on the pitch and beyond.

Talent Development

Professional sports training and coaching to identify and nurture raw talent in young people from our community.

Homework Assistance

Daily academic support ensuring our players succeed in school alongside their sporting ambitions.

Youth Safety

Keeping kids engaged in positive activities, away from drugs and substance abuse through structured mentorship.

Career Guidance

Exhibitions and career expos helping young people discover and pursue their future paths with confidence.

Motivational Speakers

Regular sessions with inspiring speakers to motivate and empower the next generation of leaders.

Parent Involvement

A community-centred approach where parents actively participate in their children's development journey.

Who We Are

About Us

Our vision, mission, and the team behind Segotlo Soccer Academy.

Vision

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders

To develop the talent and support kids in their educational programs to become better people in society — leaders who give back to their communities.

Youth Development

Structured sports programs that discover and nurture young talent from grassroots to professional pathways.

Education Support

Education and sport are inseparable. We ensure every player achieves academically alongside their sporting goals.

Community Safety

Engaged youth are safe youth. Positive activities and mentorship reduce crime and substance abuse in our community.
